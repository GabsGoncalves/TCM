Before the arrival of the Pale King, Radiance was worshiped by all creatures as a supreme deity, the blinding light and superior being. Radiance is made of essence and feeds on it, however, when Prayer arrived and showed his light to the people, the radiance was forgotten and weakened. The moth tribe stopped worshiping Radiance and this caused her to become angry and weak.

In the golden age of the kingdom, the king ordered the excavation of crystal mines, and many workers obtained their Geo with this, with this, the radiance saw an opening to invade the mind of a worker, and something began to spread that was later called of plague. Soon the entire kingdom had become infected and radiance once again became a much-worshipped being, no matter how mind-controlled it was, it had incredible power.
