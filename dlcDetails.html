Grimm's
Troup

The Grimm Troupe brings a circus troupe to Dirthmounth. The troupe has some main members, including Brumm, an accordion player, Divine, a lady who swallows fragile amulets and returns them unbroken, Grimmstead, guards who watch over the circus entrance, Nymm, an accordion player who plays a carefree melody after the Troupe is banished, and last but not least... Grimm and Grimm, Nightmare King, arrived as the leaders of the troupe and bosses. Will you banish the troupe? Or will you keep Grimm, the child?

Hidden
Dreams

Hidden Dreams is a free DLC for Hollow Knight that was released in August 2017. It adds two new bosses, the White Defender and Grey Prince Zote, as well as a new dream nail upgrade, the Dream Wielder.
The White Defender is a challenging boss that can be found in the White Palace. He is a large, armored knight who uses a variety of attacks, including a powerful charge attack.
Grey Prince Zote is a more difficult version of Zote the Mighty, who can be found in the Howling Cliffs. He is a fast and agile boss who uses a variety of attacks, including a powerful spinning attack.
The Dream Wielder is an upgrade for the dream nail that allows the player to attack enemies in their dreams. This can be used to defeat dream bosses, as well as to collect essence from enemies

Life
Blood

The Lifeblood DLC added a new area to the game, the Hive. In it, new enemies, a new boss, the Hive Knight, were added. New charms and collectibles also came, and with them came new missions and areas.

God
Master

The Godmaster DLC is a challenging and rewarding addition to Hollow Knight. It adds a new character, quest, bosses, and game mode. The new character is the Godseeker, who challenges the Knight to a series of trials. The new bosses are challenging variants of the original bosses. The new game mode is a boss rush mode
