Gray Mourner

Gray Mourner is one of the five knights of the Pale Court. During the game, we only hear stories about her, when we find her, she is crying and makes a wish... Deliver the Delicate Flower to the Tomb of her loved one.

The Destiny

Here you must deliver the delicate flower.

The Quest

The quest consists of you taking the delicate flower to the grave of the Gray Mourner's beloved, however, if you take a hit, the flower will be destroyed and you will have to go back to get another one. If you use Fast travel or dream portals, the flower is also destroyed.

You can also deliver the delicate flower to other characters, changing the game's ending and adding new cutscenes.
