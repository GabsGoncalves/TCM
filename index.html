Quirrel. Where will this feeling lead him? Quirrel helps you in some moments of the game until he fulfills his role of reaching where the strange feeling leads. He hears a call to Monomon's room, the teacher, whom he carries a mask for, even without remembering anything about his past.

Who is calling him? What connection does Quirrel have with Monomon, the teacher?

The three dreamers, the three protectors of Hollownest. In one of the Pale King's attempts to overcome and end the plague, he decided to form a seal with the power of three powerful insects, sealing the temple of the black egg where, theoretically, the plague was contained.

The dreamers are Herrah the beast, Monomon the teacher, and Lurien the observer.
