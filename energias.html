Soul

The Soul is the life force of creatures, the Soul can be stored, manipulated and stored The Soul can also be extracted from living beings, and its manipulation and release depends on the strength, knowledge and divinity of the carrier creature.

Void

Emptiness is pure, condensed power. A strong energy that has a liquid form, however, can be molded and change its physical form, to the point of obeying command orders. The Pale King used the void to create soldiers and many vessels. Memories can be recorded in this energy.

Essence

The essence is an energy that is linked to the dreams of creatures. It is manipulated like the Soul, but comes from an external source, the Dream World. This world exists in everyone's mind and is interconnected through the Essence, this allows a good user with knowledge of the essence to access the world of another creature.
