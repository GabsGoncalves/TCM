The Nailsmith

The responsible for forging your nail is called the nailsmith. His only dreams are forging the pure nail and feel the blade slicing him on a deadly strike. After finishing forging your nail for the last time he will ask you to hit him with it... Will you be capable of killing him?

The nail

During your journey looking for answers and a way to save Hallownest, your dull and worn out nail will not be enough to face the enemies on your way. You will find the Nailsmith on the left side of the city of tears and he will better out your nail for a fair price and some materials, the purest nail is only for the strongest of the knights.

Nailmasters and their Nail arts

Very long ago, there were a lot of nailmasters and nailsages, although with Hallownest fall the number of knights got smaller and now there are only three nailmasters and one nailsage left. Sly is the Great Nailsage and the brothers Oro, Mato and Sheo are the last Nailmasters left and Sly's disciples.

The Nailmaster Oro lives on the Kingdom's Edge, closed off from everyone. He distanced himself from his brothers and nailsage because of a fight with Mato.

The Nailmaster Mato lives on the Howling Cliffs, closed off from everyone. Mato resents Oro for something and says Oro owes him something he will never forget.

The Nailmaster Sheo lives on Greenpath, closed off from everyone. He decided to isolate himself to pursue a new art, the art of painting, sculpting, and creation of various artworks. Later, he befriends and the Nailsmith (if you choose to spare the Nailsmith's life)

Nailsage Sly is the last one of his kind and is said to be very powerful.
Sly is really fast and has really good combat abilities.
He teached all three brothers all they know about nail arts.
After you master all the nail arts he reveals himself and gives you the Nailmaster's Glory amulet, an amulet that shortens significantly the amount of time you need to use the nail arts.
